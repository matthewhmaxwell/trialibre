# Code of Conduct

The Trialibre project is maintained by the **American Institute for Medical Research (AIMR)**, a non-profit organization.

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Give and accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for patients and the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the AIMR project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
